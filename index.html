Project Pop Up.


Aaron L Wilson, Sherka H Wilson

Bio/Summary for Pop Up:

Pop Up is an innovative web-based startup that is revolutionizing the way users monetize their content while fostering meaningful connections with their target audiences. Our engaging platforms and tools empower content creators, streamers, modders, and users alike, offering unique opportunities to connect, collaborate, monetize, and unleash their creativity. With projects like Pop Up Feed, Pop Up Playground, and Prompt League, we are pushing the boundaries of interactive experiences, content monetization, creative competitions, and the future of online esports.

Pop Up Feed, our very own social media platform, introduces a refreshing approach to content sharing and collaboration. Through its whiteboard-based interface, users can unleash their creativity by customizing their whiteboards with a wide range of media elements such as images, videos, gifs, animations, and more. The collaborative nature of Pop Up Feed opens up possibilities for teams to work together, creating innovative and engaging content. Users can connect, collaborate, and inspire each other, fostering a vibrant community where ideas flourish.

At Pop Up, we embrace the future of online esports with our flagship project, Prompt League. Prompt League is not only a prompt battle league but also aims to become the next big esports phenomenon for online users. In Prompt League, prompters (AI and Human) compete to produce the best responses from Chat GPT and Stable Diffusion. The league consists of teams that battle it out, showcasing their prompt construction skills and creativity. We envision Prompt League as a competitive and thrilling esports event that captivates audiences worldwide.

To enhance the immersive experience, we have planned 3D avatars for prompters in Prompt League. These avatars, powered by Ready Player One technology, will enable prompters to battle inside the 3D Playground World, chat, and interact during the exciting battle events. The avatars will serve as Pop Up Playground sub-accounts, providing an integrated and seamless experience for prompters as they dive into the competitive world of Prompt League.

Join us at Pop Up and unleash your creative potential while connecting with your audience, collaborating with others, participating in creative competitions, and embracing the future of online esports. Whether you're a content creator, streamer, modder, or digital enthusiast, our platforms and tools offer endless possibilities for engagement
